1. All participants must be medically cleared before competing.

2. Both competitors must wear approved safety gear including gloves and a mouthpiece.

3. All competitors must be of similar size, weight, and physical condition - weight classes.

4. No intentional strikes to the throat, spine, or back of the head.

5. No biting, spitting, or hair-pulling.

6. No intentional headbutting, kicking, or stomping.

7. No non-approved holds or submission attempts.

8. No striking a downed opponent.

9. All fights must take place in a regulated, approved cage.

10. The referee may pause or stop the fight at any time.

11. No intentional fouls or illegal techniques.

12. The referee must declare a winner based on the rules of the match.

13. All competitors must adhere to the rules of the match.
