Beer Pong

Category: Casual Players: 2-4 per team Equipment: Ping pong balls, cups, table

Beer pong, also known as Beirut, is the staple game of US college parties. It has evolved into a competitive sport with its own world series, combining skill, strategy, and social elements.

Basic Setup

Equipment

  • Ping pong balls
  • 6-10 cups per side
  • Standard table

Team Structure

  • 2-4 players per team
  • Competitive format: 2 players

Official Rules

  • Teams take turns throwing ping pong balls into opponents' cups
  • Cups are arranged in a triangle formation
  • When a ball lands in a cup, that cup is removed
  • First team to eliminate all opponent's cups wins

World Series of Beer Pong

The World Series of Beer Pong VIII featured 160 teams competing for a $50,000 prize pool. The competition allows water as an alternative to beer, emphasizing skill over consumption.
