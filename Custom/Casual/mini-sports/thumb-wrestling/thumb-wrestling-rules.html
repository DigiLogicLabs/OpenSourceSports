Thumb Wrestling

Category: Mini Sports Players: 2 Also Known As: Pea-knuckle, Thumb War

A classic hand-to-hand competition where two players attempt to pin each other's thumbs. Recently formalized into organized competitions by the World Thumb Wrestling Association.

Game Origins

Multiple origin claims exist, including:

  • Paul Davidson's grandfather (family claim)
  • Julian Koenig (childhood invention)

Official Rules

Starting the Game

"1, 2, 3, 4, I declare a thumb of war"

Victory Conditions

  • Pin opponent's thumb
  • Declare victory with traditional chant
  • Winner declares: "1, 2, 3, 4, I win thumb-o-war"

Competition Format

The World Thumb Wrestling Association organizes official competitions, bringing formal structure to this casual game.
