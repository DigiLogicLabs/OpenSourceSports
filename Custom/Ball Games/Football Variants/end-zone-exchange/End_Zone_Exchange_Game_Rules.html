End Zone Exchange

Category: Ball Games Players: 2 Equipment: Football

A strategic and athletic game that combines throwing accuracy and catching prowess, where players alternate between throwing and catching to score points through skillful catches.

Field Setup & Equipment

Playing Field

  • Flat, open area
  • Two marked points 25 yards apart
  • Clear of obstacles

Equipment Needed

  • 1 football
  • Field markers

Scoring System

Catch Type Points
Normal catch 1 point
One-hand catch 2 points
Jump catch 3 points
Volley catch Additional points (pre-agreed)

Game Rules

Thrower Rules

  • Must keep feet planted
  • Throw until catch or ground contact
  • Moving feet results in 1 point penalty

Catcher Rules

  • Limited steps allowed
  • No hands during volley
  • Immediate catch after volley
