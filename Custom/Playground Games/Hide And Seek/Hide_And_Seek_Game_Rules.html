Hide and Seek

Category: Playground Games Players: Teams of 5-7 Equipment: None required

A competitive version of the classic hiding game, featuring organized tournaments and international variations. This traditional children's game has evolved into a structured sport with specific rules and competitions worldwide.

International Variations

Japanese Version (NSSA Rules)

  • 7 players per team
  • 10-minute matches
  • 65ft x 65ft pitch (adults)
  • 55ft x 88ft pitch (under 12)
  • 5-minute halves
  • 2-minute hiding time

Italian Version (Nascondino)

  • 5 players per team
  • 60-second counting period
  • 10-minute search time
  • Central target system
  • Neutral searching team

Tournament Structure

Officials

  • 2 referees
  • 1 game coordinator

Format

  • 4 groups
  • 2-day competition
  • Progressive elimination
